You Should Know

Over 75% of serious domestic violence injuries and deaths occur AFTER the victim has decided to, or attempted to leave the relationship.

You may be especially at risk if your partner

  • Owns a gun
  • Has threatened to kill you
  • Has a substance abuse problem
  • Has abused you worse or more often lately
  • Has threatened you in any other way (taking your children, etc.)
