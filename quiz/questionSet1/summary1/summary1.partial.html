It's Not Your Fault

You are more likely to experience domestic violence if you
  • Are young
  • Are introverted or isolated and have few friends
  • Are unemployed
Your partner is more likely to become violent if they
  • Are drug- or alcohol-dependent
  • Have a history of abusive behavior
  • Were physically or psychologically abused as a child
Your relationship may lead to abusive behavior if
  • There are strict gender roles
  • You are experiencing economic stress
  • There are many relationship conflicts
