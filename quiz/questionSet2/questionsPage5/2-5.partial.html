Section 9: Physical Abuse

EMERGENCY ESCAPE
Destruction of Property

My partner is destructive when he/she is angry.

Physical Discipline

My partner physically disciplines my/our children against my will.

Physical Control

My partner grabs me, holds me down, or stops me from leaving.

Physical Abuse

My partner pushes, hits, kicks, strangles or throws me.

Physical Aggression

My partner makes me afraid he/she will be physically violent.
