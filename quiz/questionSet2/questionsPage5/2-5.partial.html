Section 2: Physical Abuse

EMERGENCY


My partner hits things, throws things, or breaks things in front of me when he/she is angry with me.

My partner physically disciplines my/our children against my will.

My partner pushes, shoves, grabs me, holds me down, or holds me hostage in rooms or vehicles.

My partner slaps, hits, punches, kicks, strangles or throws me.

My partner chases me, yells at me, invades my space in threatening ways, or otherwise makes me feel afraid.
