Section 8: Verbal Abuse

EMERGENCY ESCAPE
My partner calls me names, insults me, or criticizes me often.

My partner is very critical of me in front of other people.

My partner threatens to hurt me, my children, my family, or my pets.

My partner tells me that I am lucky to have him/her, that I will never find anyone better.

My partner yells at me and/or others when he/she is angry.
