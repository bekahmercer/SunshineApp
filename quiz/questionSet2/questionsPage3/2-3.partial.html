Section 7: Emotional Abuse

EMERGENCY ESCAPE
Isolation

I can't spend time with friends and family because of my partner.

Punishment

My partner emotionally punishes me by witholding affection.

Blaming

My partner blames me for his/her bad behavior.

Cheating

My partner cheats on me.

Gaslighting

My partner tells me I am crazy and makes me feel like I might be.
